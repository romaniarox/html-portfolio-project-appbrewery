The Best Movies According To Roshani

My top 3 movies of all time.

Zootopia

A movie which will inspire to never give upon your dreams and be the change you have always wanted to be in this world.


Baby's Day Out

The way a small baby goes on an adventure and outsmarts the very people who plan to kidnap him.


Chak! De India

A movie which will instil a sense of patriotism for your country as well as make you fall in love with the sport of hockey.
